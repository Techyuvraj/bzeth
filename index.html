Our Company

At BZETH, we are a specialized biopharmaceutical company dedicated to addressing the critical health challenges caused by digestive enzyme deficiencies. Our core mission is to improve quality of life for patients suffering from pancreatic insufficiency and other enzyme-related disorders through innovative, high-quality enzyme therapies.

With deep expertise in the development, manufacturing, and commercialization of animal-sourced and recombinant digestive enzymes, we offer a targeted portfolio that includes solutions for exocrine pancreatic insufficiency (EPI), cystic fibrosis, chronic pancreatitis, and other gastrointestinal conditions.

Our R&D and manufacturing teams are at the forefront of enzyme technology, combining scientific rigor with advanced bioprocessing capabilities to ensure high potency, purity, and stability of our products. We are actively working with our partners to develop novel formulations and delivery systems that enhance enzyme activity and patient adherence.

Backed by a commitment to quality, compliance, and patient care, BZETH collaborates with healthcare providers, researchers, and global partners to expand access to life-improving enzyme therapies. Every step of our journey is driven by a simple goal: to support healthier digestion and better lives.

The Founders

At the heart of BZETH are our founders, Dr. Vaishali Rawal and Dr. Chandramauli Rawal, whose shared vision, scientific expertise, and deep commitment to patient care form the foundation of our company.

Dr. Vaishali Rawal

Co-Founder

Dr. Chandramauli Rawal

Co-Founder

With combined expertise spanning medicine, pharmaceutical sciences, biotechnology, and clinical research, Dr. Vaishali Rawal and Dr. Chandramauli Rawal founded BZETH with a clear mission: to provide high-quality, life-changing digestive enzyme therapies for individuals suffering from enzyme deficiencies like exocrine pancreatic insufficiency (EPI), cystic fibrosis, and other gastrointestinal disorders.

Their philosophy is simple yet profound: science should serve people. With decades of practical experience in drug development, regulatory affairs, and biomanufacturing, they bring a unique perspective that bridges the gap between cutting-edge research and real-world patient needs.

Their leadership inspires innovation, integrity, and excellence throughout our organization—from research and development (R&D) to production and patient support. They are building more than just a company; they are building a legacy of care, rooted in purpose and progress.
